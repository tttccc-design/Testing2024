BACKGROUND:

The comfortable and convenient life accompanied by the fantastic growth of science and technology can account for the overwhelming rubbish. The more developed the region is, the more rubbish there is.

GOAL:

Rubbish-handling is a quite complex issue, and it needs cooperative efforts from every person. Thus, we want to make an application which educates people to handle their daily rubbish in the right way and develop their habits to classify the garbage.
